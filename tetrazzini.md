Turkey Tetrazzini
Ingredients (for 1.5 lbs of spaghetti)

[ Note: this recipe uses a total of 8 oz butter ]

- 1 lb mushrooms
   4 Tbsp butter (2 oz)
   1–1½ bell peppers
   2 oz olive oil
   2–3 oz sherry
   4 Tbsp butter (2 oz)
   4 Tbsp flour						
   12 oz milk
   6 oz chicken stock
   3 oz cream
   2 egg yolks
   1.5 lb spaghetti
   Turkey, diced
   1/2 lb white cheddar cheese, grated

Directions

   1 - Sauté the mushrooms in 4 Tbsp of butter. Add 2–3 oz of sherry and cook briefly to combine.
   2 - Sauté the bell pepper(s) in 2 oz olive oil until softened.
  
   3 - Prepare the béchamel – cook 4 Tbsp butter and 4 Tbsp flour to a roux - add milk (12 oz) and chicken
   stock (6 oz) – cook until smooth - season - mix 2 egg yolks with 3 oz cream – temper the egg/cream mixture
   with 4 Tbps of the warm sauce – add the tempered egg/cream mixture - cook gently until the sauce “rises”
   slightly against the spoon when stirred - NEVER allow to boil.
   
   4 - Cook the spaghetti.
   5 - Combine the spaghetti, mushrooms with sherry, peppers, béchamel, and diced turkey.
   6 - Spoon the spaghetti mix into a large, buttered baking dish, sprinkle with ½ lb cheddar cheese, bake 325   
   for 20 minutes.
